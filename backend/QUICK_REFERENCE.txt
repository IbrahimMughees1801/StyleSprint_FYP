## 📱 Connect Phone

1. Get PC IP: `ipconfig` → Find IPv4 (e.g., 192.168.1.100)
2. Update Flutter: `lib/services/virtual_tryon_service.dart` line 11
3. Change to: `'http://192.168.1.100:8000'`
4. Run: `flutter run`
